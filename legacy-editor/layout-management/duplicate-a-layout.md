# Duplicate a Layout

From the **list of layouts** that have been customized and achieved in App Dashboard > **Select a Layout** > Click **Duplicate Template.**

<figure><img src="/.gitbook/assets/dublicate-layout.png" alt="" width="1050"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You will have **2 identity Layouts** with the same settings. Then you can modify one from them and keep the other as needed.

<figure><img src="/.gitbook/assets/dublicate-layout1.png" alt="" width="1050"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
