# Product Wishlist

**Wishlist** is a collections of desired products saved by customers to their user account, signifying interest without immediate intent to purchase. Offering **Wishlist** is an effective way to reduce shopping cart abandonment and fulfill sales from customers who showed intent but didn't end up purchasing.

Let's install **Nitro Wishlist App** to level up your store now. The App is very light-weight and easy to use.
