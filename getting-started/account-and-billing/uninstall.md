# Uninstall LayoutHub app

## Overview

In this article, you will learn about how to uninstall LayoutHub and what will happen to your LayoutHub pages and section if you uninstall the app from your Shopify store.

By following this article, you can uninstall LayoutHub without encountering any unexpected obstacles.

## LayoutHub Uninstall Agreement

You must agree to the uninstallation terms before proceeding:

{% hint style="warning" %}
By uninstalling, you agree to the following terms and accept responsibility for what happens after uninstalling LayoutHub from your store

**1. Data Removal**

* Any pages and sections created using LayoutHub will be trashed and permanently deleted after 30 days.
* Uninstalling LayoutHub will not remove all LayoutHub code in your theme.&#x20;
* Previous pages and sections you created with LayoutHub in your theme and live store will still work normally.

**2. Subscription and Billing**

* Your LayoutHub subscription will be terminated immediately, thereby ending any contractual obligations between you and LayoutHub.
* Billing and charges may continue until the end of your current Shopify billing cycle, as governed by Shopify's App Billing Cycle Policies.
* Any pricing policies, discounts, special offers, or promotional rates managed by LayoutHub will end.
{% endhint %}

**Before proceeding, please note:**

* The uninstall process will automatically terminate your subscription; Shopify will handle all billing matters according to Shopify's policies.
* Backing up your created pages, sections, or any important content you may want to use later. Please check [this article](../../page-and-section-management/export-import.md).
* You can follow [this article](change-your-plan.md) to downgrade your plan to the Free plan instead of uninstalling.

## Alternative to Uninstalling

**If you're unsure about permanently removing LayoutHub**, consider these alternatives:

1. **Downgrade to Free plan**: If cost is a concern, you can downgrade to continue using LayoutHub with 1 publishable page/section. How to downgrade to Free plan? Please check [this article](change-your-plan.md).
2. **Archive important content**: Export and save your pages and sections before uninstalling, please check [this article](../../page-and-section-management/export-import.md).

{% hint style="warning" %}
**Important**: If you uninstall LayoutHub and later reinstall the app, you'll be subject to current pricing plans. Previous pricing arrangements, discounts, or grandfathered rates will not be restored.
{% endhint %}

## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. Is it possible to restore data after uninstalling LayoutHub?

**Yes, you can restore data after uninstalling within 30 days.**

After the uninstallation process is complete, all pages and sections you created will be moved to the **Trash folder**. You can restore them within 30 days, after 30 days all data will be permanently deleted.

How to restore page and section form Trash? Please check [this article](../../page-and-section-management/detele-and-restore-deleted-template.md).

### 2. What happens to my live store after uninstalling LayoutHub?

**All pages and sections created with LayoutHub still work normally.**

Don’t worry, all the pages you’ve created and published in your theme and live page will still work as normal. However, you won’t be able to edit them after uninstalling LayoutHub.

### 3. What about my subscription and billing?

* Your LayoutHub subscription is terminated immediately upon uninstallation.
* You may continue to be charged until the end of your current Shopify billing cycle (this is managed by Shopify's billing system, not LayoutHub).
* Any special pricing, discounts, or promotional rates will be permanently lost.
* If you reinstall LayoutHub later, you will be subject to the pricing plans in effect at the time of your installation.

### 4. How do I remove all LayoutHub code from my theme?

Removing code requires direct changes to your theme and can be complex. We don’t recommend doing this on your own, please contact us for support. To get support, please check [this article](../../faq-and-common-issues/faq/how-do-i-get-support.md). 

If you want to remove all LayoutHub code by yourself, please check [this article](uninstall.md#id-4.-how-do-i-remove-all-layouthub-code-from-my-theme).
