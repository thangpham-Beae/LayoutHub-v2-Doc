# What is LayoutHub?

{% embed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=-1I4OV53yRE" %}

**LayoutHub is Shopify’s easiest page builder, designed for every merchant — no technical skills required.**

With LayoutHub, you can create a beautiful, high-converting store by simply selecting a template and editing it. **No coding, no drag-and-drop — just pick, edit, and publish.**

Built with the mission of **helping merchants launch better e-commerce experiences**, LayoutHub makes it effortless to set up your store and boost your sales.

## Why LayoutHub?

**1. Easy to Use:** No learning curve, no complex options.&#x20;

**2. The central hub of every eCommerce layouts:** find all layout you need&#x20;

**3. Built-in Advanced Features:** Everything you need to optimize your store to rocket your sale. All in one place

## Key Feature&#x20;

<details>

<summary>Easy Editor</summary>

LayoutHub is designed to be the easiest page builder for Shopify. The editor follows the Shopify Customizer, so there’s no learning curve. You don’t need to drag and drop, avoiding messy layouts or performance issues. Options are kept minimal and focused on essential, easy-to-understand adjustments—no complex or abstract settings. Instead of building from scratch, you can simply choose a template and customize it, saving time and ensuring consistent, high-quality results.
