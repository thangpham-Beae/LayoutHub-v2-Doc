# Preferences

## Overview&#x20;

**Preferences** is the central place to manage all settings related to your LayoutHub app. From here, you can review your plan details, access analytics, manage account and store settings, manage theme configurations, enable theme app extensions, and handle code-related tasks.&#x20;

This section helps you control how LayoutHub integrates with your store and ensures everything is configured to match your needs.

3. Account settings&#x20;
4. Store settings&#x20;
5. Theme management&#x20;

7. Add code to your theme&#x20;
