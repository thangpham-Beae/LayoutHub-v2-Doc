# Insert section

## **1 - Plans**

### **1.1 - With free plan:**&#x20;

You only can insert section to top and bottom of standard page.

![](</.gitbook/assets/image (137).png>)

### **1.2 - With Basic plan:**&#x20;

You can insert section to top and bottom of standard page, product, collection, collection, Blog, Article templates.

![](</.gitbook/assets/image (291).png>)

### **1.3 - With Pro and Premium plans:**&#x20;

You have all authority of free and basic plan but you can insert section to any where you want on Home page. ( This page only display when you set it like default)

![](</.gitbook/assets/image (113).png>)

You can easy insert some sections to any position in your home page  just by click and select. Now let follow the video to learn more.
