## 1 - Shopify Review App

Layout Hub Team has built appearance for Review sections of Shopify Review App on the Product Page. It's very fit to entire page's design. However if you want to manage and analytic customer feedbacks. You need to have a **Review App** to do that. You need to choose App to use by this way: **Go to Settings > Third party app settings > Review apps**

After that you click to **Save**
